How My Ratings Work

When reviewing a movie, TV show, or videogame, I will give them a rating out of ten:

10/10 Awesome! A favorite of mine.

9/10 Really good.

8/10 Good, but has some problems

7/10 Enjoyable, has some good moments.

6/10 Not bad, just nothing incredible.

5/10 Painfully mediocre.

4/10 Plain not good.

3/10 Garbage

2/10 Hot garbage.

1/10 Steaming pile of crap.

0/10 You didn't even try.
